Introduction to Cats

Cats are popular household pets known for being playful, independent, and intelligent.

They come in many breeds and often enjoy relaxing in warm, quiet spaces.

Fun Facts About Cats

Interesting Behaviors

  • Cats sleep between 12–16 hours per day.
  • They have highly sensitive hearing.
  • Cats can jump up to six times their body length.

Basic Care Steps

  1. Provide balanced nutrition daily.
  2. Ensure access to clean, fresh water.
  3. Schedule regular veterinary visits.

Health Tip

Providing scratching posts and interactive toys keeps cats physically active and mentally stimulated.
