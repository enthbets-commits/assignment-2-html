Introduction to Cats

Cats are popular household pets known for being independent, playful, and intelligent.

They come in many breeds and enjoy relaxing in warm spaces.

Fun Facts About Cats

Interesting Behaviors

  • Cats sleep 12–16 hours per day
  • They have excellent hearing
  • Cats can jump six times their body length

Basic Care Steps

  1. Provide balanced nutrition
  2. Ensure access to clean water
  3. Schedule regular vet visits

Cat Photo

Health Tip

Provide toys and scratching posts to keep your cat active and mentally stimulated.
