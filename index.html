Introduction to Cats

Cats are popular pets known for being playful and independent.

They come in many breeds and enjoy resting in warm, quiet places.

Fun Facts

  • Cats sleep most of the day
  • They have sharp senses
  • They are great jumpers
  1. Feed your cat daily
  2. Give clean water
  3. Visit the vet
